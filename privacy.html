Privacy Policy

Last updated June 17, 2026

Recorso is built to be private. It does not collect, store, or share your personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Recorso has no account and no sign-in. It includes no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. We do not gather any information about you or how you use the app.

Your data stays on your device

Every day, label, and note you enter is stored only on your device. This information is never sent to us or to anyone else, and we have no way to see it.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive your name, payment details, or any other personal information from that purchase. Apple's handling of the transaction is covered by Apple's own privacy policy.

Deleting your data

Because your data lives only on your device, removing the app deletes it. We keep no copy and cannot recover it.

Children

Recorso does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the date at the top of this page will be updated to reflect it.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@borbole.com.
