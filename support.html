Support

A few quick answers. If you are still stuck, send us a note and we will help.

How does the free trial work?

Recorso is free to use in full for 14 days. After that, a one-time unlock keeps it going. There is no subscription.

Is it a subscription?

No. The unlock is a single one-time purchase. No subscription, no ads, ever.

I unlocked it, but I am on a new phone or reinstalled the app.

Open the app and tap Restore Purchase on the unlock screen. Your unlock is tied to your Apple ID, so it carries over at no extra cost.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. There is no account and no cloud. One thing to know: deleting the app erases your tracked days, so they are kept only within your device's own backups.

How do I track two things at once?

Open settings with the slider icon and add a second tracker. You can then compare two variables side by side, such as mood against sleep.

How do I rename or recolor a label?

In settings, tap a label to rename or recolor it, or swipe left on it to delete it. Changes save automatically.

Still need help? Email Support. We usually reply within a couple of days.
